Senatus verdict

Weighing merit and peril

Pros

  • Day length close to Earth’s — familiar rhythm.
  • Water traces and geology that once flowed with rivers.
  • Thin air eases ascent compared to dense worlds.

Cons

  • Radiation unshielded — no global magnetic umbrella.
  • Dust storms that cloak the globe for months.
  • Too cold and thin for unaided human breath.
